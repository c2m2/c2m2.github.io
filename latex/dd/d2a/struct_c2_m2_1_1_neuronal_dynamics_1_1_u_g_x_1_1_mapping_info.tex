\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Encapsulates the grids and mapping data 

\mbox{\hyperlink{struct_c2_m2_1_1_neuronal_dynamics_1_1_u_g_x_1_1_mapping_info}{Mapping\+Info}}

Geometries will be stored in Model\+Geometry (1d geometry) and Surface\+Geometry (2d geometry) The dictionary maps any 2d surface vertex, using its index in the 2d mesh, to two 1d vertices with their associated indices in the 1d mesh and a lambda parameter.
