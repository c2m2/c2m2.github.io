Detailed Description

Encapsulates the grids and mapping data

MappingInfo

Geometries will be stored in ModelGeometry (1d geometry) and SurfaceGeometry (2d geometry) The dictionary maps any 2d surface vertex, using its index in the 2d mesh, to two 1d vertices with their associated indices in the 1d mesh and a lambda parameter.
